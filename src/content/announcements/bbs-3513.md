次の例会は，2月22日（日）の午前９時〜午後４時です。場所は北大植物園入り口向かいの10階建てビル「かでる2.7」（札幌市中央区北2条西7丁目）の8階「810A室」です。
見学も歓迎しますが、ゲームを行なう場合は、筆記道具（メモ、ボールペン等）をご用意ください。9時頃に１ラウンドの組合せをし、２ラウンド以降の開始時刻は不定ですので、ゲームをされる方は9時頃に来ていただくのが良いです。

Our next regular meeting will be held on Sunday, February 22, from 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.
The venue is Room 810A on the 8th floor of “Kaderu 2.7,” the 10&#8209;story building located across from the entrance of the Hokkaido University Botanical Garden (Kita 2-jo Nishi 7-chome, Chuo-ku, Sapporo).
Visitors are welcome.
If you plan to play games, please bring your own writing materials (such as a notepad and a pen).
We will make pairings for the first round around 9:00 a.m.
The starting times for the second and subsequent rounds are not fixed, so if you wish to participate in the games, it is best to arrive around 9:00 a.m
