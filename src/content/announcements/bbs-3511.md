次の例会は，2月8日（日）の午前９時〜午後４時です。場所は北大植物園入り口向かいの10階建てビル「かでる2.7」（札幌市中央区北2条西7丁目）の8階「810A室」です。
見学も歓迎しますが、ゲームを行なう場合は、筆記道具（メモ、ボールペン等）をご用意ください。9時頃に１ラウンドの組合せをし、２ラウンド以降の開始時刻は不定ですので、ゲームをされる方は9時頃に来ていただくのが良いです。

Our next regular meeting will be held on Sunday, February 8, from 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.
The venue is Room 810A on the 8th floor of “Kaderu 2.7,” the ten&#8209;story building located across from the entrance of the Hokkaido University Botanical Garden (Kita 2&#8209;jo Nishi 7&#8209;chome, Chuo&#8209;ku, Sapporo).
Visitors are welcome. If you plan to play games, please bring writing materials such as a notebook and pen.
We will make pairings for the first round around 9:00 a.m., and the starting times for later rounds will vary. Therefore, those who wish to play are encouraged to arrive around 9:00 a.m.
