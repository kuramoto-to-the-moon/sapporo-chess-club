次の例会は，12月7日（日）の午前９時〜午後４時です。場所は北大植物園入り口向かいの10階建てビル「かでる2.7」（札幌市中央区北2条西7丁目）の8階「810室」です。
見学も歓迎しますが、ゲームを行なう場合は、筆記道具（メモ、ボールペン等）をご用意ください。

The next meeting will be held on Sunday, December 7, from 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.
The venue is Room 810 on the 8th floor of “Kaderu 2.7,” a ten-story building located across from the entrance to the Hokkaido University Botanical Garden (Kita 2-jo Nishi 7-chome, Chuo-ku, Sapporo).
Visitors are welcome. If you plan to participate in any games, please bring writing materials (such as a notepad and a ballpoint pen).
